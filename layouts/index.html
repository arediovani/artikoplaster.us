Our Services

A wide range of decorative finishes to meet your needs

Venetian Plaster

Classic Italian technique creating smooth, polished marble-like surfaces with depth and luminosity.

Marmorino

Traditional lime-based plaster with a natural stone appearance and exceptional durability.

Tadelakt

Waterproof Moroccan plaster perfect for bathrooms and wet areas with a silky, soapstone finish.

High Polish Marble Finishes

Luxurious, mirror-like finishes that capture and reflect light beautifully.

Multi-Layered Textures

Decorative Wall Treatments

Custom designs including stenciling, limewash paint, and artistic applications.
